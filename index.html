Our approach

One Team, From Design to Done.

Most renovations go sideways in the handoffs — between the designer and the builder, between one trade and the next, between what was promised and what actually got built.

We do it differently. Argyle Building Company manages your entire project under one roof and one contract, so you're never chasing separate contractors or wondering who's responsible. We uncover and showcase the character that makes your house worth keeping, then build in the modern comforts you want around it — all with a single point of contact from first sketch to final walkthrough.

Services

What We Build

  • 01

    Whole-Home Renovation

    Bringing a dated house fully up to your standard — layout, systems, and finishes, room by room, under one coordinated plan. The right move when the location is perfect but the house isn't.

  • 02

    Kitchen Remodels

    The most complex room in the house, done right: cabinetry, countertops, plumbing, electrical, ventilation, and finish work coordinated by one team so you're not managing five vendors yourself.

  • 03

    Bathroom Remodels

    Spa-grade primary baths, guest baths, and powder rooms — with the waterproofing and substrate work beneath the tile that separates a bathroom that lasts from one that doesn't.

  • 04

    Home Additions

    More space that looks original, not bolted on — new wings, second stories, bump-outs, and in-law suites, matched seamlessly to your existing home and engineered to code.

  • 05

    Basement Finishing

    Turning raw square footage into real living space — media rooms, gyms, guest suites, and offices — with the moisture management and egress that finishing a basement correctly demands.

  • 06

    Outdoor Living

    Decks, patios, porches, and outdoor kitchens that extend your living space outdoors, combining carpentry, masonry, and electrical into one finished result.
